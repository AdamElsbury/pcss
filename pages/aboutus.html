Our mission.

Provide Project planning expertise to support your delivery success.

Who are we?

Project Controls and Support Services (PCSS) Ltd is a small business situated in Bridgend, South Wales. We have been in business for over 12 years and have worked with 10 fantastic companies so far.

PCSS is a people driven company who strive to help organise and plan your professional projects and we love seeing successful projects thrive. We don't believe in taking over a project, we firmly believe that the best projects are completed by collaboration.

We will sit down and listen to your goals and KPI's, and draft up the most effective plan possible to meet your needs.

We understand sometimes a business needs a push in the right direction.

We're happy to help.

Our process.

So what makes us tick? What can you expect once you bring us onto a project? We're proud of the process we have refined with over 12 years of hard work.

STEP 1

Meet with you to discuss the scope, deliverables, budget & constraints.

STEP 2

Present the project schedule to the stakeholder & obtain acceptance of the schedule baseline.

STEP 3

Execute the work in line with the schedule, monitor & report progress against the baseline.
