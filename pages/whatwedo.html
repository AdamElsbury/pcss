Our mission.

Provide Project planning and management assistance to allow your projects to perform as efficiently as possible.

Who are we?

PCSS is a small business situated in Bridgend, South Wales. We have been in business for over 12 years and have worked with 10 fantastic companies so far.

PCSS is a people driven company who strive to help organise and plan your professional projects and we love seeing successful projects thrive. We don't believe in taking over a project, we firmly believe that the best projects are completed by collaboration.

We will sit down and listen to your goals and KPI's, empathize with your needs and draft up the most effective plan possible.

We understand sometimes a business needs a push in the right direction.

We're happy to help.
